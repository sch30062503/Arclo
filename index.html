How it works

From sign-up to prize money
in four steps

01
Create your account

Sign up once with your username and region. One profile covers every game on Arclo — no need to register separately.

02
Enter a tournament

Browse open tournaments, pick your game and division, and enter. Bronze is always free. New tournaments start regularly.

03
Play your weekly match

Every week you get a fixture. Play on the scheduled match day, submit your result, and the standings update automatically.

04
Rise and get rewarded

Finish top 3 in your tournament and get offered promotion to Silver. Win at Gold level and take a share of the prize pool.

Divisions

Everyone starts in Bronze

Bronze

The starting point. Open to everyone, no entry fee, no experience needed. Play weekly fixtures, earn points, and compete for promotion.

Free to enter
Silver

Earned through promotion from Bronze. Small entry fee, stronger competition, and a prize pool paid out to top finishers each tournament.

Entry fee applies
Gold

The elite tier. The biggest prize pools, the strongest players, and a path to The Circuit Finals — Arclo's annual showpiece event.

Entry fee applies

Platform features

Built for competitive play

Multiple games, one profile

Enter tournaments across different games without creating new accounts. Your stats are tracked per game, per division.

Fixed weekly match days

No scheduling headaches. Every tournament has a set match day and kick-off time. Log in, play, submit your result.

Fair dispute system

If scores don't match, both players upload evidence. Admin reviews and makes the final call. No result goes unresolved.

Live standings

Tournament leaderboards update in real time after every match. See exactly where you stand and what you need to promote.

No waiting around

New tournaments open regularly. If one is full, another is starting. Miss a promotion? Play again next tournament.

Promotion and prizes

Top 3 in every Bronze tournament get offered free Silver entry. Win at Silver or Gold and take real cash prize money.
