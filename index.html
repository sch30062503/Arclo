How it works

From sign-up to prize pool
in three steps

01
Sign up and get placed

Create your profile, pick your game, and join Bronze league for free. Everyone starts here — no experience needed.

02
Compete every week

Get a weekly fixture against a player in your division. Set your availability, agree a time, play your match, and submit the result.

03
Rise and get rewarded

Win matches, earn points, and climb the table. Top players promote to Silver and Gold — where the prize money is.
